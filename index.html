Why I enjoy playing and watching volleyball

  • Volleyball to me is a very interesting and enjoyable sport to not only play but to watch as well.



Playing

  1. The first reason why I have always enjoyed playing volleyball is because out of all of the sports I have played it seems to be the only one I have actually loved playing
  2. The second reason I love volleyball is because there is just something so simple in my eyes about it.
  3. The third reason is because I love the feeling of being around others who also enjoy it and support me even when I mess up and I love being able to tell others it's okay, or they will get it next time.


Watching

  1. I like watching volleyball because I can relate to the players and it just sucks me in, especially when they get a good rally going it's hard to look away.
